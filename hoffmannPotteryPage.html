Hoffmann Pottery

Hoffmann Pottery

Client: Colter Hoffmann

Technologies: Visual Studio Code, HTML5, CSS, SASS, Squareup

Take a Look: https://illustrious-scone-23abf4.netlify.app/

Project Description: I met Colter Hoffmann about a year ago. One day he showed me his work and how he only sells his projects through instagram or people that know him. He mentioned that he wanted a website but did not want to go through the work to make one. At the time I did not have much practice so now that I have had some more experience, I feel like I can make him a website that he will be pleased with.

This website will be used to sell pottery that is made by Colter Hoffmann. There will be a Homepage that will show the different types of pottery being sold such as mugs, bowls, vases, cookware etc. Those who visit this website will be able to click the different cards/images of the pottery being sold and once clicked they will be taken to a new page that displays the rest of the category they have chosen. Once they are at a new page of the specific category they will be able to add items to their shopping cart. There will also be a Gallery page which will show some of the pottery sold or will be sold. There will be an About Us page to tell what this website is about and how it got started. The last page will be a Contact Us page where the user can contact the company about any questions or concerns they may have.
